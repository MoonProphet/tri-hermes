# Fate/Hollow Ataraxia

Compiled text dump for the Hollow Ataraxia english translation, compiled by DestinyBlade

[Hollow Ataraxia is best experienced by playing the VN yourself!](https://forums.nrvnqsr.com/showthread.php/7705-Fate-Hollow-Ataraxia-Voice-Patch?p=#2741240)

Due to the cyclical nature of this VN, the pages are distributed in terms of Loops, and repeated scenes are omitted with only the scene title placed.

In a future update, repeated scene headers will redirect you to where the scene is first transcribed.
